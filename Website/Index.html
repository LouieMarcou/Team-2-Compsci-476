Corner Side help

Just people helping people here.

Did you know that more than 500,000 thousand people are homeless in America alone. Approximately around 35% of these people abuse alcohol and other substances. This causes a dilemma. It creates the question of "Do I give them money or are they just going to use it for drugs." Through Corner Side Help we are allowed to answer this question for you. We have created a card that allows these people to accept your donations through this website and only allows them to be used at fast food chains and some local restaurants. Thus, providing a meal for those most in need without the fear of the money being misused.
